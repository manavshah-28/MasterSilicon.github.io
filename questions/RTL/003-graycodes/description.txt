## Design modules for converting binary to gray code and gray to binary

### Decimal, Binary and Gray equivalents
- Decimal: 000 Binary: 000 Gray: 000 
- Decimal: 001 Binary: 001 Gray: 001
- Decimal: 002 Binary: 010 Gray: 011
- Decimal: 003 Binary: 011 Gray: 010
- Decimal: 004 Binary: 100 Gray: 110
- Decimal: 005 Binary: 101 Gray: 111
- Decimal: 006 Binary: 110 Gray: 101
- Decimal: 007 Binary: 111 Gray: 100
